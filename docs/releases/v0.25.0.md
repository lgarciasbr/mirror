# v0.25.0 — Soul Mode More Voices

**Date:** 2026-06-08

## Highlights

- Adds Wisdom Voice as a full active Soul Mode listening lens.
- Adds Beauty Voice as a full active Soul Mode listening lens.
- Keeps both new voices inside the ritual card under `the voice says`.
- Removes `listening for` from Wisdom and Beauty cards so the voice can occupy the card fully.
- Requires situated `--says` content for Wisdom and Beauty to prevent placeholder cards.
- Adds canonical prompts for Wisdom Voice and Beauty Voice.
- Updates the Pi Soul Mode skill so natural-language validation can hear the new voices.
- Preserves the non-mutating Soul Mode boundary: no automatic journal, identity, or project-state changes.

## Where We Started

The first Soul Mode release proved the ritual cycle, but it left a visible gap:
Possible Listenings could name Wisdom Voice and Beauty Voice, while the user
could not yet hear them as active rites. That made the constellation feel larger
than the behavior available inside the product.

The next release therefore focused on the missing voices rather than on
integration, rich UI, or psyche mutation. Before Mirror can ask what should be
integrated into identity, the user needs to hear the voices the ritual already
implies.

## What Changed

Wisdom Voice is now hearable. When the user chooses it, Mirror renders a `♢
WISDOM VOICE LISTENING` card and places the full wisdom utterance inside the box
under `the voice says`. Wisdom Voice is not generic advice and not a short
summary. It is the voice of a relevant source — a thinker, philosopher, sacred
text, ancient tradition, proverb, contemplative teaching, mythic image, or wisdom
text — speaking from its own symbolic world.

The Wisdom card now omits `listening for`. This was an important ritual
correction: the card is not a form with fields. It is the place where the source
speaks. Mirror's practical bridge happens after the card, in Mirror's ordinary
tone. When Mirror knows the source, author, work, or historical/textual context,
it should explain that origin outside the card and relate it to the user's
conversation without inventing bibliographic precision.

Beauty Voice is also now hearable. When the user chooses it, Mirror renders a `✺
BEAUTY VOICE LISTENING` card and places the full beauty utterance inside the box
under `the voice says`. Beauty Voice listens through image, texture, rhythm,
atmosphere, body, literature, poetry, music, and the form of aliveness. It does
not decorate pain, force hope, or turn difficulty into a pretty lesson.

Beauty also omits `listening for`. Its card contains the aesthetic listening
itself; Mirror's bridge follows outside the card. When a poem, author, artwork,
song, or aesthetic source is known and relevant, Mirror may name it outside the
card, but should not fabricate citation details.

Both new voices require situated `--says` content when rendered through the CLI.
This prevents the runtime from displaying placeholder text when the assistant has
not yet composed the actual voice response from the user's material.

## Conscious Exclusions

- No Closing Rite or psyche-layer integration is included.
- No automatic Self, Shadow, Ego, persona, or journey identity enrichment is included.
- No identity mutation is performed.
- No rich web Soul Mode UI is included.
- No Passagem curation or fragment library is included.
- No Return To Center implementation is included.
- No multiple-fruit model is included.
- Soul Mode still does not execute implementation work; operational mutation requests route to Builder Mode.

## What We Learned

The key refinement was that a voice card must contain the voice itself, not a
label plus an explanation outside the box. Wisdom Voice in particular needed a
stronger separation between the source speaking inside the card and Mirror
interpreting outside it. Once that boundary was clear, Wisdom could become
solemn, symbolic, and ancestral without turning Mirror's normal bridge into a
prophetic performance.

Beauty followed the same grammar. It should not be a motivational consolation or
aesthetic gloss. It should let form, texture, rhythm, image, and care appear
inside the field, while leaving the practical connection to Mirror's ordinary
voice outside the card.

The release also reinforced a process lesson: pre-release refinement does not
need to become a separate implementation story when real validation closes the
open questions inside DS1 and DS2. The planned refinements story was cancelled
because the necessary tuning happened during direct Pi validation of the two
voice stories.

## Next Horizon

The next Soul Mode horizon is integration: a Closing Rite, review of harvested
material, and safe psyche-layer enrichment proposals with visible diffs and
explicit confirmation. That work should remain careful and non-automatic because
it touches the Mirror's living identity model.
